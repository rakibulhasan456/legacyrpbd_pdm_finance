Terms and Conditions
  • Finance Duration: The Buyer agrees to clear the full vehicle price within the selected finance duration of weeks from the agreement date.
  • Failure to Complete Payment: If the Buyer fails to clear the full payment within the agreed duration, a court notice will be issued. After receiving the notice, the Buyer will have an additional 1 week to clear all dues along with a late fee of $5,000.
  • Vehicle Seizure: Failure to clear payment after the additional 1-week period will result in permanent seizure of the vehicle by the Dealership.
  • Non-Refundable Payments: Any payment made is strictly non-refundable.
  • Lawyer Fee: A mandatory lawyer processing fee of $5,000 applies.
  • Vehicle Recovery: If available after seizure, recovery requires a fee of $10,000 plus full outstanding balance.
  • Legal Binding: This Agreement is legally binding under applicable law. Any dispute arising from this Agreement shall be resolved according to applicable law.
